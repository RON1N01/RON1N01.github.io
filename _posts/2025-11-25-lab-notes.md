## Technical Work

### VMware Hanging Process Fix
- VMware GUI would not open even though background process was active.
- Verified with:
  ```bash
  ps aux | grep vmware
  ```
- Solution that restored UI:
  ```bash
  killall -9 "vmware-vmx"
  ```
